Welcome to your AI-driven Assistant!

This app is designed to help you plan and manage your semester courses efficiently. Explore various strategies for course selection, prioritize your must-take courses, and use the integrated chatbot for quick assistance. To start using the full functionality of the app, please make sure to fill in your "Institution Details" under the "Account" section. This step ensures access to the app's primary features, including course selection and chatbot interactions.

Strategy
